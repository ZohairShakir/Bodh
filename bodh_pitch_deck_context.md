## 1. Product Overview
- **Name:** Bodh AI
- **Tagline:** The Study Tool Your Exams Deserve.
- **Elevator Pitch:** Bodh AI is a high-speed, zero-friction AI study platform built specifically for Indian engineering and degree college students. It instantly transforms raw lecture notes, chapter excerpts, and PDFs into structured summaries, multiplayer competitive quizzes, and smart glossaries—all in under 8 seconds. 

## 2. The Problem
- **Information Overload:** Students struggle to parse through 400-page textbooks and disorganized lecture notes before exams.
- **Isolation & Lack of Engagement:** Studying is traditionally a solitary, monotonous task with little immediate feedback on mastery.
- **Generic AI Tools:** Existing AI chatbots operate in a generic Q&A format and are not optimized for the specific syllabus structures, exam patterns, or subjects (like Thermodynamics, Data Structures, Fluid Mechanics) taught in Indian academic institutions.

## 3. The Solution
Bodh AI acts as an instant academic synthesizer and gamified study companion. A student simply pastes their notes, and Bodh builds a clean, highly structured study pack. It removes the friction of studying by structuring the data exactly how a "topper" would write it, and gamifies the review process through competitive duels with batchmates.

## 4. Core Features & Capabilities
1. **Instant Synthesis Engine:** Generates clean, topic-by-topic bulleted summaries that emphasize logical flow and clarity, rather than machine-like text blocks.
2. **The Quiz Arena (Multiplayer Duels):** Transforms any static document into a competitive landscape. Students can challenge their batchmates to real-time 1v1 duels based on the generated MCQ quizzes to see who mastered the material first.
3. **Contextual AI Tutor:** A floating, always-available AI assistant that provides contextual help. Students can ask the tutor to clarify specific topics or bullet points directly within their study pack without losing their place.
4. **Smart Glossaries:** Automatically extracts key technical terminology and definitions, building an exam-ready vocabulary instantly.
5. **Team Collaboration & High-Compression Sharing:** Study packs can be shared instantly via ultra-lightweight URLs (using lz-string compression) or synchronized in a real-time Team Chat lobby. 
6. **Zero Friction & Privacy First:** Users can experience the core value without a forced login. Notes are not stored long-term and are cleaned after the session, ensuring complete privacy.

## 5. Target Audience & Market
- **Primary Market:** Indian college students, specifically in rigorous degree programs like Engineering (B.Tech, BE), Computer Science, and foundational sciences.
- **User Persona:** High-intent students looking for efficient last-minute exam prep, and competitive students who want to test their knowledge against peers.

## 6. Competitive Advantages (The "Secret Sauce")
- **Gamification:** The multiplayer Quiz Arena turns exam prep into a social, competitive event, driving higher retention and organic growth through peer-to-peer sharing.
- **Hyper-Local Focus:** Tailored UI/UX and feature set designed specifically for the Indian academic ecosystem.
- **Premium User Experience:** A stunning, modern "glass-metal" aesthetic featuring violet and teal themes, interactive neural animations, and micro-interactions that make the platform feel premium and engaging, unlike sterile academic tools.
